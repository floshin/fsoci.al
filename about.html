About fsocial

fsocial takes a stand against established 'social media', which are asocial in design. We give a fuck about their addictive and harmful mechanisms and think that social media has to be non-commercial and free of ads on principle. We encourage people who have something to express to have their own website instead of a social media profile. To simplify this process we work on an app automating website creation and give the control back to the host: Yourself.

Public websites are presented in a feed in form of a simple index webpage, like an illustrated list of all those who decide to boycott established social media to join the fsocial network.

If you want your already existing webpage to be included, all you have to do is to comply to basic standards of non-abusive webdesign.
